Get Started
icon-clock.png 6-8 months before

Form a Science Fair Committee

▾

Recruit teachers, administrators, parent volunteers, and community members to help organize the event.

  • Identify a lead coordinator
  • Recruit 5-10 committee members
  • Assign roles: logistics, judging, registration, prizes
  • Set up regular meeting schedule
2

Set Goals and Scope

▾

Determine the size, grade levels, and categories for your science fair.

  • Define participating grade levels
  • Decide on individual vs. team projects
  • Choose science categories (Biology, Physics, etc.)
3

▾

Book a location with enough space for displays, judging, and visitors.
